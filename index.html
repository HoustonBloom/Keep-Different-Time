Why keep different time

01

When people say time is "moving too fast" ‚Äî that's not a personal failing. That's the calendar working as designed.

02
Celestial time is the one clock nobody owns.
The moon doesn't have a shareholder. The solstice doesn't have a marketing team. These rhythms predate every empire, every economy, every institution. No one can buy the full moon. No one can move the equinox to maximize Q4 revenue. Choosing celestial time is a quiet, structural act of refusal.

03

How to start

/// How to Start ///

  1. Download the calendar

    A printable template mapping lunar phases and solar feasts for the year. Pin it up. That's the entire infrastructure.

    This isn't a movement. It's a rhythm. It works when it's shared with the people you're actually trying to hold close. Start with one.

    You don't have to do all of it. Notice which phase the moon is in. Let the question it carries sit with you. The rhythm does the relational work that individual willpower can't sustain.
